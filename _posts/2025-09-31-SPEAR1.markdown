We build SPEAR-VLM, a 3D-aware vision-language model that infers object coordinates from a single image, and use it to train SPEAR-1, a robotic foundation model with grounded 3D perception and language-conditioned control. SPEAR-1 trains on ~45M frames from 24 Open X-Embodiment datasets, matching or surpassing Pi0-FAST and Pi0.5 while requiring ~20x fewer robot demonstrations; weights and 3D-annotated datasets are slated for public release.
